Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

About NDT

The G7 Aerospace NDT Team is responsible in conducting inspections for all F/A-18D Hornet aircraft belonging to the Royal Malaysian Air Force (RMAF) as part of the Local Planned Maintenance (LPM12Y) program.

G7 Aerospace's NDT department has been certified by the Civil Aviation Authority of Malaysia (CAAM) in September 2023 to conduct four specific types of NDT inspections.

Testing Methods

Explore our comprehensive range of non-destructive testing methods.

Eddy Current Testing

  • Surface inspection, tubing inspection, and array inspection.

Ultrasonic Testing

  • Contact testing with direct probe placement.
  • Dolphicam2 for advanced diagnostics and precision imaging.

Penetrant Testing

  • Fluorescent penetrant examination with color contrast.
  • Fluorescent penetrant examination with fluorescent agents.

Magnetic Testing

  • Color contrast magnetic particle testing.
  • Fluorescent magnetic particle testing with fluorescent agents.
